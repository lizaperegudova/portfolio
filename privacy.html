Privacy Policy

Overview

This is a personal portfolio website. I respect your privacy and only collect anonymous data to understand how visitors use the site.

What I collect

I use Google Analytics to track basic visitor statistics — pages viewed, country, device type, and how you found the site. This data is anonymous and aggregated; I cannot identify individual visitors.

I do not collect any personal information, do not run ads, and do not sell or share data with anyone other than Google (the analytics provider).

Cookies

Google Analytics uses cookies to distinguish users and sessions. These cookies are first-party and store an anonymous identifier — no personal data.

If you'd prefer not to be tracked, you can:

  • Install the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on
  • Use your browser's "Do Not Track" setting or a privacy-focused browser
  • Block cookies for this domain in your browser settings

Your rights

Under GDPR and similar regulations, you have the right to request access to, correction of, or deletion of any data related to you. Since this site only collects anonymous analytics, there is no personal data tied to you specifically.

If you have any questions, reach out via Telegram.
